Note for legal review: These estimates are based on KBRA's observed YouTube traffic patterns and Brightcove's actual encoding profile for this account. The projections use a standard fixed 720p H.264 bitrate of 1,800 kbps and include a 3× buffer on regular traffic to account for organic growth and unpredictable spike events. Even with a 5× growth scenario (62,500 plays, ~5.7 TB), KBRA remains comfortably within both contract limits. Bandwidth overages — the only metric with potential overage charges — would require usage to increase by more than 440× from current projected levels.
