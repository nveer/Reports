Current generally available (GA) and pilot features for What's Next Media

Features Overview
Auto-Captions Already Active GA

Automatically generate accurate captions in 90+ languages

Generate natural-sounding captions across 90+ languages. Includes a Custom Dictionary for controlling how brand names, acronyms, and product terms appear — reducing manual edits and ensuring consistency.

  • 90+ language coverage
  • Custom Dictionary for brand terminology control
  • VTT, SRT, and embedded caption formats
  • Improved accuracy with advanced speech recognition
  • WCAG 2.1 AA / ADA compliance support
Universal Translator Discussed on Call GA

Localize videos into 50+ languages with AI dubbing

Automatically localize video content into 50+ languages with AI-generated transcripts, translations, and audio dubbing. Refine transcripts before translation, then choose between synthetic voices or speaker-modeled voices that match the original speaker.

  • 50+ language translation support
  • Transcript refinement before translation
  • Synthetic voice dubbing
  • Speaker-modeled audio (preserves original speaker tone)
  • Multi-audio track support
  • End-to-end localization workflow in Video Cloud
AI Content Multiplier Discussed on Call GA

Turn one long-form video into multiple short-form assets automatically

Convert long-form content into short-form clips, highlight reels, vertical video formats, and social-ready assets — with auto-generated metadata including titles, descriptions, and hashtags. What used to take hours of manual editing is done in minutes.

  • Automated clip and highlight generation
  • Horizontal → vertical format conversion
  • Highlight reel creation
  • Auto-generated metadata (titles, descriptions, hashtags)
  • Social-ready output (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Facebook)
  • Maintain brand standards across all output clips
Metadata Optimizer GA

Auto-generate and translate metadata from video content

Automatically generate and translate metadata by analyzing video content — text, audio, and visuals. Creates titles, descriptions, tags, and keywords that improve discoverability and streamline content management. Supports 40+ languages with customizable style and tagging rules.

  • Auto-generate titles, descriptions, tags, keywords
  • Multi-language metadata translation (40+ languages)
  • Visual and audio content analysis
  • Customizable style and tagging rules
  • Faster content ingestion workflows
